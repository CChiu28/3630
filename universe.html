The Expanse


The universe. The space that governs all of our matter and physics. It is never ending, and still expanding. It is larger than our human minds can concieve. Billions of years old, we are but an extremely small part of this grand space and we still know next to nothing about what's out there.

The Reach


Bigger than we can ever imagine, the universe still expands to this very moment. With an approximate radius of 13.7 billion lightyears, countless stars ebb and flow through the dark.
