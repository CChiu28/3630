Black Holes

Previous Next

Over the course of your life time, you may heard of the term "black hole". Throughout media, you may have seen these black holes depicted in science fiction and in video games. In the past decades, it was believed they were possibly a worm hole to another dimension or another part of space, but it is now believed they are nothing but a pure destructive part of the universe. Nobody knows what they really look like and nobody knows what really lies beyond them.

What are they?

Massive and dense, black holes are the carcasses of dead stars and celestial bodies collapsed onto itself.

How big are they?

They vary from size. As small as planet Earth to larger than our sun, black holes are a force to be reckon with regardless of their size.
