Stars

Light


Stars... they bring the light to our universe. They grow until they run out of the materials they need to sustain themselves and then they either explode into a fantastic display of gases or they implode unto themselves and become a black hole. These titans among us give us the spectacle of our night sky. Together, they form the beautiful galaxies that illuminate the dark space around us. They are the lifegivers of our universe.

Titans Among Us


As gases bunch together and explode in chemical reactions, they burn and illuminate the space. They continue to grow as long as their bodies contain the elements needed for combustion. They are among the biggest astronomical bodies in space, dwarfing our little planet by magnitudes.
